App Description:
Stomp Covid Rumors is a COVID-19 tracking app that allows students on campus to track rumors of COVID infections in their campus community. Students can post about news of COVID-19 cases and provide details on location. They can interact with other studentsâ€™ posts by up-voting and down-voting rumors that may be false and even track past rumors through the search page based on location. Those officials in the health department or in Resident Life can also be active on the platform to monitor rumors and debunk rumors. They would have administrator privileges to approve/disapprove rumors as long as they're logged in with the right credentials.
